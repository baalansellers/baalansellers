### 👨‍💻 About me

I build and maintain software at **Rev.io**, working across the back end, front end, and the
infrastructure that ties it together. I like untangling messy problems, automating the boring
parts, and shipping things that hold up in production.

When I step away from the keyboard, I'm usually:

- 📻 **Ham radio** — chasing contacts and tinkering with antennas
- 🤖 **Combat robotics** — building BattleBots-style fighters
- 🖨️ **3D printing** — prototyping parts (often for the robots)
- 🎨 **Model building & painting** — slow, satisfying, detail-obsessed work
- 🎲 **Board games** — always up for a new one
